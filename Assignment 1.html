AVENGERS

The Avengers is a group of our beloved superheroes that exist in not only in the Marvel Cinematic Universe but also our hearts. Originally created by Late Stan Lee in his comics, these heroes have shaped the childhoods of not only our parents' but also ours' when the Russo Brothers adapted it on to the big screen. The original 6 Avengers were Captain America, Iron Man, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow and Hawkeye. Let us now learn a little about the origin of these characters prior to Avengers.

  1. CAPTAIN AMERICA

    Steve Rogers aka Captain America was a would-be U.S. Army enlistee rejected by recruiters because of his small size. He volunteers to receive a top-secret serum and transforms into a “super-soldier.” Dubbed Captain America and clad in a red, white, and blue costume with a matching stars-and-stripes shield, Rogers joins the army.

  2. IRON MAN

    Tony Stark aka Iron Man was charismatic and eccentric, he was also very proud, but also very altruist and heroic. He did not have a good childhood, and this affected his actions. Stark was very smart, and a very talented inventor. His technology is always very advanced, and Iron Man remains to be his greatest piece of work. His heart was critically injured by shrapnel, and he was captured by the communist Wong-Chu, who demanded that Stark build him a weapon. Stark instead built a suit of armor that sustained his heart, becoming Iron Man.

  3. THOR

    Thor Odinson aka God of Thunder is a prince and warrior. Thor was short-tempered and strong-willed – two characteristics that his father believeed would prevent him from being a good king. After risking the lives of fellow Asgardians, his father stripped him of his hammer, Mjolnir, and banished him to Earth.

  4. HULK

    Bruce Banner aka Hulk is a scientist who was exposed to heavy doses of gamma radiation during an experiment gone wrong transformed into the mean, green rage machine we now know as Hulk. Bruce Banner lives a life caught between the soft-spoken scientist he's always been and the uncontrollable green monster powered by his rage.

  5. BLACK WIDOW

    Natasha Romanoff aka Black Widow was skilled in many forms of martial arts including aikido, judo, karate, savate, and boxing. In addition, she was an amazing gymnast, and used her acrobatics to gain an edge in combat. Her fighting skills were augmented by a variant of the Super-Soldier serum, which she was given as part of the Black Widow program.

  6. HAWKEYE

    Clint Barton aka Hawkeye is a master marksman and was longtime friend of Black Widow. While Clint Barton has no superhuman powers (with the exception of the period when using Pym particles as Goliath), he is at the very peak of human conditioning. He is an exceptional fencer, acrobat and marksman, having been trained from childhood in the circus and by the criminals Trick Shot and Swordsman.
